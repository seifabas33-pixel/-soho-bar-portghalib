Our Story

Where Italian tradition meets Egyptian soul

Our kitchen is born from the meeting of two worlds — Italian tradition and Egyptian authenticity — united in one identity that tells our story.

"Every day we craft our bread by hand for our tapas and our burgers, using selected ingredients and techniques that honour every detail."

From the long-risen dough of our Neapolitan pizza to high-quality Italian ingredients, every plate is made to offer an authentic experience. Fresh pasta, creamy risottos, prime Angus fillets and our local specialty — the Camel Steak — complete a menu that blends tradition, craft and the flavours of the Mediterranean.
